Your control

You can use the site without providing any Personal Information. If you choose not to provide any Personal Information, you may not be able to use certain parts of the service, web-based mobile app, and website. For example, to use the Workshop Café web-based mobile application, you currently need to log in with your Facebook account and/or provide your mobile phone number.

Exclusions

This Privacy Policy does not apply to any Personal Information collected by Workshop Café other than Personal Information collected through the service, web-based mobile app, and website. This Privacy Policy shall not apply to any unsolicited information you provide to Workshop Café through any means. This includes, but is not limited to, information posted to any public areas of the site, app, or store, any ideas for new products or modifications to existing products, and other unsolicited submissions. All such information shall be deemed to be non-confidential and Workshop Café shall be free to reproduce, use, disclose, distribute and exploit it without limitation or attribution.

Children's information

Workshop Café does not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not submit any Personal Information through the Service, web-based mobile app, and website. We encourage parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce our Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide Personal Information on this service, web-based mobile app, and website. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 13 has provided Personal Information to Workshop Café through our service, web-based mobile app, or website, please contact us and we will endeavor to delete that information from our databases.

The service, web-based mobile app, and website may require you to sign in with your Facebook account to access logged-in features. When you log in using Facebook, we will collect relevant information necessary to enable the service, web-based mobile app, and website to access Facebook, and Facebook will provide us with access to certain information that you have provided to it. We will use, store and disclose such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. However, please remember that the manner in which Facebook uses, stores and discloses your information is governed by its policies, and Workshop Café shall have no liability or responsibility for its privacy practices or other actions.

To help you quickly set up a profile, the service, web-based mobile app, and website may import some of your data from Facebook and add it to your Workshop Café profile, including but not limited to your Facebook profile picture(s). These data may be visible to other Workshop Café users, regardless of whether those users are your friends on Facebook. You can edit certain parts of your Workshop Café profile in the settings.

You may at times in the future have the option of posting your Workshop Café activities to other social networking service, web-based mobile app, and website like Facebook or Twitter. You acknowledge that if you choose to use this feature, your friends, followers and subscribers on any social networking service, web-based mobile app, and websites you have enabled will be able to view such activity.

You also acknowledge that other Workshop Café users may mention you in their posts, notes, and/or encounter summaries, and that such mentions may be shared on Facebook.
